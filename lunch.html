Fresno EBT Map

Helping Fresno access hot meals with EBT.

About This Map

This directory lists restaurants in the Fresno area that participate in the Restaurant Meals Program (RMP). This program allows elderly, disabled, and homeless individuals to use their EBT benefits to purchase prepared hot meals.

How to Use

Browse the pins on the map above to find a location near you. Click on a pin to see the full address and business name.

Updates

This list is generated from official public records. Always verify with the restaurant before ordering.
