Tutoring Options


Kid's Tutoring Grade K-10

When I tutor kids, I take having fun very seriously. I throw puzzles and games and riddles at them which develop their reasoning faculty. Math problems in disguise essentially. We can be doing MineCraft and exploratory Algebra the next.


Adults Grade 11-Undergrad

I also tutor adults and high school students. For these students I focus on the repair of a broken foundation, and I try to get the students to do the work of exploration themselves. I also prioritize the understanding of specific subjects too, to help with the stresses of exam time.
